The Objective of HUMSJ

Haramaya University Muslim Students Jema'a (HUMSJ) is a student-led organization that aims to promote Islamic values and principles among the students of Haramaya University. The organization provides regular Islamic education, encourages community service, and creates a safe and inclusive environment for all students. HUMSJ aims to produce a sincere and honest generation that is academically talented, ethically disciplined, and exemplary.

The Objectives of HUMSJ in detail →

  • Praying the obligatory prayers on time
  • Reading your portion of the Quran
  • Reciting morning and evening remembrances (azkar)
  • Performing the sunnah prayers after obligatory prayers
  • Performing the witr prayer, with a minimum of one rak'ah, and the recommended amount being three rak'ahs
  • Performing the duha prayer, with a minimum of two rak'ahs and no limit to the maximum
