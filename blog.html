Goal, Vision and Mission of HUMSJ in detail

Vision:

To empower the Muslim students' jema'a at Haramaya University, elevating it to become the pinnacle of university jema'as in the country, while providing comprehensive support and services to both students and the local community, fostering a harmonious and flourishing environment.

Mission:

Our mission is centered around cultivating the intellectual and spiritual growth of students, striving to nurture well-rounded individuals who excel in both their Islamic knowledge and secular education. We are dedicated to creating a nurturing atmosphere that encourages active involvement in social services and encourages students to become remarkable role models, inspiring others through their compassion and commitment to their community.

Goal:

Our ultimate goal is to empower a generation of sincere, ethical, and academically accomplished individuals who embody unwavering dedication and exemplify excellence. By instilling values of integrity, discipline, and dedication, we aim to equip them with the necessary skills and knowledge to serve Islam and their country, ultimately leaving a lasting positive impact on society. Together, we will embark on this transformative journey, nurturing talented individuals to become pillars of their communities, embracing the teachings of Islam, and making a profound difference in the world around them.
