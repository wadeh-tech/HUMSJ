The Goal, Vision and Mission of HUMSJ

Vision

To make haramaya university muslim students' jema'a the leading university jema'a in the country and to provide full services for both students and the local community

Mission

To work toward creating healthy and competent citizen to increase his knowledge and affiliation by using various options to enrich the students with islamic knowledge as well as secular education. working toward creating conducive environment for students to participate in various social services and increasing their community engagement by enhancing them to be a magnificent role model to others.

Goal

To produce a sincere and honest generation that's talented academically educated, ethically disciplined and examplary who is matured enough to serve islam and his country

The Objectives

  • To provide regular Islamic education and guidance to the students through various programs such as lectures, seminars, and workshops.
  • To encourage students to participate in community service and volunteer activities to enhance their social responsibility and leadership skills.
  • To establish a strong network with local organizations and institutions to promote mutual cooperation and collaboration.
  • To organize various extracurricular activities such as sports, arts, and cultural events to create a well-rounded student experience.
  • To provide counseling and support services for students to address their academic, personal, and spiritual needs.
  • To create a safe and inclusive environment for all students regardless of their background or beliefs.
  • To promote research and innovation in the field of Islamic studies and related disciplines.
  • To collaborate with other universities and organizations to promote interfaith dialogue and understanding.
